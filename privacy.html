Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Overview

Pocketnori ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Instagram automation service at pocketnori.com (the "Service").

By using Pocketnori, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Service:

Account information

  • Your email address, collected when you join our waitlist or create an account.
  • Your name, if you provide it during sign-up.

Instagram account information

  • Your Instagram Business or Creator account ID and username, obtained through Instagram's official Graph API after you explicitly grant permission.
  • Instagram post metadata (post IDs, captions, timestamps) required to run your automations.
  • Comment and direct message data — only the specific content needed to execute the automation rules you configure. We never read or store private messages beyond what is necessary to deliver your chosen automation.

Usage and technical data

  • Automation execution logs (which rules ran, when, and their outcome).
  • Device type, browser type, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

Payment information

  • We use a third-party payment processor. We do not store your full card number or banking details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Create and manage your Pocketnori account.
  • Execute the Instagram automations you configure within the Service.
  • Send you transactional emails (account updates, subscription notices, support replies).
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, we do not build profiles for sale, and we do not share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Pocketnori does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, ever.

How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing) who process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Instagram / Meta: We interact with Instagram's official API on your behalf. Your data is subject to Meta's own privacy policy for any information exchanged through their platform.
  • Legal requirements: If required to comply with a law, regulation, or valid legal process.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you cancel your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage, HTTPS for all data transmission, and access controls to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time by cancelling your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pocketnori.com.

Children's Privacy

Pocketnori is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
